WHAT IS MEGAPONT
APE CLUB?

Megapont Ape Club is a celebration of the more scenic members of the Megapont world. Due to accelerated evolution, they have inherited many behaviours of the closely related human species. Both the good and bad.

Chimpanzees have evolved at an accelerated rate due to a genetic mutation and as such is considered the kingmakers within Megapont. They currently reside outside of Megapont plaza and live in the idyllic paradise outside the metropolis.

MEGAPONT ROBOTFACTORY

The Megapont Robot Factory has been discovered, apes are busy getting the factory operational for a completely customisable and interactive mint process.

Megapont Apes can claim one empty robot, also known as a mainframe and a one-component crate. You can give your robot a name and trade them, along with components on the marketplace.

MEGACOIN

MEGACOIN is the token for the web3 community on Stacks. MEGA is the governance token for Megapont and the community initiatives that move the space towards a better, more decentralised future.

The claim period for MEGA opens in May for holders of Megapont Apes, Robots and Components. With the claim for MegaKongs opening shortly after.

MegaKongs

MegaKongs are an expansion of the Megapont Ecosystem on the Ethereum blockchain, launching April 11th 2022

MegaKongs will carry full membership in Megapont. The utility will be similar to Megapont Apes but proportional to our stance that “Apes are Kings”.
